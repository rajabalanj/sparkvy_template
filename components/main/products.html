Nickel Foam Details

Product Name: Nickel Foam

Description: Nickel foam is a highly porous metallic material characterized by its interwoven, three-dimensional cellular structure and porosity exceeding 95%. This unique morphology imparts an extensive surface area and excellent electrical conductivity, making it indispensable in advanced energy and environmental technologies. Its mechanical robustness, chemical stability, and tailored pore architecture enable widespread applications in batteries, supercapacitors, fuel cells, catalysis, and acoustic insulation. As both a structural and functional material, nickel foam continues to play a pivotal role in driving innovation across multiple high-performance engineering domains.

Graphite Sheet Details

Product Name: Graphite Sheet

Description: Graphite sheets are high-performance materials known for their exceptional thermal conductivity, chemical resistance, and flexibility. Composed of over 95% pure exfoliated graphite, these sheets are widely used in industries such as automotive, petrochemical, electronics, and energy. Ag/AgCl Electrodes Details

Product Name: Ag/AgCl Electrodes

Description: The Ag/AgCl reference electrode is a widely used and reliable electrochemical reference system ideal for both laboratory and field applications. Constructed from a silver wire coated with silver chloride and immersed in a stable electrolyte such as saturated potassium chloride, it delivers a consistent and reproducible potential. As a mercury-free alternative to traditional reference electrodes, it supports environmentally conscious practices while offering excellent performance across a broad pH range. With applications in pH measurement, potentiometric titration, and advanced techniques like cyclic voltammetry, the Ag/AgCl electrode remains a cornerstone in scientific research and industrial analysis.
