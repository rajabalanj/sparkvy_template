About SARI

SparkVy Advanced Research and Innovations (SARI) have established by a team of researchers who have well expertise in materials science, nanotechnology, synthesis and analysis for a wide range of applications like energy storage devices, energy conversion, sensors, biomedical, water splitting, and water treatment. The mandate of our organization is to develop materials and processes of industrial importance. Since characterization is an essential part of the study and development of materials, SARI has a dedicated Centre of Excellence with high-end sophisticated facilities for materials characterization and testing, the core focus of this Centre being microstructure-structure- property correlations. Salient Features of SARI

  • Each facility is operated by well-experienced researcher, often at the site of the manufacturer.
  • Our research experts will help users in the analysis and interpretation of data as may be needed.
  • All of the instruments are maintained by way of Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC).
  • Calibration is carried out regularly, often as part of the AMCs.

Mission

Our mission is to advance science and technology through pioneering research that addresses real-world challenges. We are committed to fostering innovation, nurturing interdisciplinary collaboration, and delivering high-impact solutions that contribute to societal progress, economic growth, and sustainable development.

Vision

To become a global catalyst for transformative discovery and innovation, fostering an interdisciplinary ecosystem where curiosity meets purpose. We envision a future where our research breakthroughs drive societal progress, improve quality of life, and inspire the next generation of scientific pioneers.

Note: Since the facilities of SARI are tuned to applications in materials science, we are not able to take up work on biological samples at the present time (till September 2025).
