About Us

SparkVy Advanced Research and Innovations have established by a team of researchers who have well expertise in materials science, nanotechnology, synthesis and analysis for a wide range of applications like energy storage devices, energy conversion, sensors, biomedical, water splitting, and water treatment. The mandate of our organization is to develop materials and processes of industrial importance. Since characterization is an essential part of the study and development of materials, SparkVy has a dedicated Centre of Excellence with high-end sophisticated facilities for materials characterization and testing, the core focus of this Centre being microstructure-structure- property correlations.

SparkVy also offers Hands on Training, internships for students and faculties those who are interested to working on supercapacitors, water splitting, batteries, solar cells and anti-bacterial applications.

Mission

Our mission is to advance science and technology through pioneering research that addresses real-world challenges. We are committed to fostering innovation, nurturing interdisciplinary collaboration, and delivering high-impact solutions that contribute to societal progress, economic growth, and sustainable development.

Vision

To become a global catalyst for transformative discovery and innovation, fostering an interdisciplinary ecosystem where curiosity meets purpose. We envision a future where our research breakthroughs drive societal progress, improve quality of life, and inspire the next generation of scientific pioneers.
